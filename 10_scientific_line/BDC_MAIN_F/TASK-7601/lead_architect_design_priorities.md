# Lead Architect Design Priorities

1. Preserve the canonical R1 interpretation: selection-physics reboot only.
2. Preserve the two-way gate semantics: `PASS_TO_R2` or `REMAIN_IN_R1`.
3. Preserve the legacy control as a comparator, not as the only admissible future regime.
4. Use BDC Designer as a bounded analytical reviewer over the finished result, not as a source of broader claims.
5. Treat a positive BDC review as permission for R2-scoped implementation work only.
