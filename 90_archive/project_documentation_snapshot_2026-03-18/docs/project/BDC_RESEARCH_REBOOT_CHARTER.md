# BDC Research Reboot Charter

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-03-17  
**Status:** ACTIVE CHARTER  
**Depends On:** `docs/project/BDC_PROJECT_HISTORY_MAP.md`, `docs/project/BDC_RESEARCH_REBOOT_PLAN.md`

---

## 1. Charter Purpose

This charter opens the next scientific line of BDC after the freeze of `BDC Designer`.

Its job is to prevent an undisciplined return to the old organism line.

The charter defines:
- what the reboot is trying to do,
- what historical assumptions are closed,
- what remains scientifically alive,
- what must be validated before any new organism-level claims are allowed.

---

## 2. Charter Statement

BDC Research is hereby reopened as a **new scientific line**.

It is not a continuation of the old `hidden_rule` product path.

It is a rebooted research program with:
- preserved determinism,
- preserved reproducibility,
- preserved negative-result discipline,
- and a new requirement that selection physics must be validated before organism architecture is expanded.

---

## 3. Preserved From Historical BDC

The following are explicitly preserved:
- quaternary uncertainty as a valid computational design question,
- DNA-inspired memory as a valid systems question,
- resource-bounded adaptive digital organisms as a valid long-term target,
- transfer and cooperation as valid higher-order hypotheses,
- `EDP1` as historical evidence and laboratory baseline,
- reproducibility/kill-criteria/append-only governance,
- negative results as first-class evidence.

---

## 4. Explicit Closures

The following are explicitly closed for the reboot baseline:

1. `hidden_rule` as a direct product direction
2. legacy linear complexity penalty as accepted biology-like selection physics
3. any return to universal transition-law claims before bounded mechanism validation
4. any “build SuperCell now and test later” execution style

These closures remain in force unless a future ADR supersedes them with new evidence.

---

## 5. Relationship To `BDC Designer`

`BDC Designer` remains the active operational line.

Its role relative to the reboot:
- provide product/cash/evidence continuity,
- provide architecture-analysis support,
- provide disciplined packet/evidence formats where useful.

`BDC Designer` is not the reboot itself.
The reboot must still stand on direct experiments.

---

## 6. Reboot Mission

The mission of the reboot is:

to re-establish a scientifically credible path from the original BDC principles toward adaptive digital organisms, but only through validated selection physics and controlled task environments.

This mission is narrower than the early project rhetoric and intentionally more disciplined.

---

## 7. Primary Reboot Questions

The reboot is authorized to investigate these questions:

1. Can a new resource/energy/complexity regime permit architectural growth without the old structural impossibility?
2. Can a controlled post-`hidden_rule` task environment preserve causal interpretability while being richer than the old toy task?
3. Can minimal organism components be validated independently under that regime?
4. Can those validated components later be recombined into a minimal adaptive cell line?

All broader claims are deferred.

---

## 8. Non-Negotiable Reboot Rules

1. No organism-level expansion before Phase R1 passes.
2. No new “cell” vocabulary without formal implementation semantics.
3. No product claims from reboot benchmarks.
4. No post-hoc threshold tuning.
5. No masking of failure through new abstractions.
6. No mixing of client workflow artifacts with scientific evidence.

---

## 9. Authorized Immediate Work

The reboot is authorized to start with:

### R0
- historical closure package,
- baseline preservation,
- reboot charter,
- reboot experiment framing.

### R1
- selection physics rebuild package,
- regime candidates,
- explicit old-regime comparison,
- feasibility and non-collapse criteria.

No higher phase is authorized until R1 is evaluated.

---

## 10. First Gate

The first real gate of the reboot is:

**Selection Physics Rebuild**

Pass condition:
- at least one new regime is mathematically feasible for architectural growth,
- and empirically avoids immediate collapse under bounded multi-seed testing.

Fail condition:
- no regime satisfies those criteria.

If fail:
- no organism assembly work starts.

---

## 11. Charter DOD

This charter is considered active only if:
- it is committed,
- the reboot plan is already committed,
- the first experiment package for Phase R1 is defined,
- and no contradictory claim remains active in project docs.

---

## 12. Bottom Line

BDC Research is reopened, but under a new contract:

- no nostalgia,
- no hidden continuation of the broken line,
- no early organism inflation,
- no universal theory rhetoric ahead of evidence.

The reboot starts with physics, not with symbolism.
